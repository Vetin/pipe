# Feature Contract Checklist

- Motivation, actors, goals, non-goals, and risks are explicit.
- Ambiguity score is recorded, with blocking questions resolved or carried as
  explicit blockers.
- Functional and non-functional requirements have stable IDs.
- Acceptance criteria are testable and map to behavior.
- Related existing features or a no-existing-solution finding are source-backed.
- Permission, audit, rollback, idempotency/replay, and observability
  expectations are present when relevant.
- Assumptions and open questions are recorded.
- `execution.md` includes `Docs Consulted: Feature Contract`.
